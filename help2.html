    Getting Started

    Installation

    1. Download the Installer: Visit our official website and download the installer for your operating system (Windows/Mac).
    2. Run the Installer: Double-click the downloaded file and follow the on-screen instructions to install the application.
    3. Launch the App: Once installed, you can launch the app from your desktop or start menu.

    Navigating the App

    Home Screen

    • Home: Access a variety of activities,comics and videos designed to teach different moral values and behaviors directly from the home screen.
    • Resources: Click on the resources section to explore websites and additional materials.
    • Help: Access this help page and other support options.

    Features

    Comics

    Comics with relatable activities and videos are placed at the very top of the screen.

    Educational Activities

    Games designed to develop specific skills such as critical thinking, honesty, and knowledge.

    Videos and Tutorials

    A collection of videos that teach important life lessons and behaviors.

    Using the Features

    Accessing Activities

    1. Select an Activity: From the home screen, click on the activity you want to start.
    2. Follow Instructions: Each activity comes with detailed instructions to guide you through.

    Watching Videos

    1. Select a Video: On the video section, select a video to watch. Videos are categorized by topics presented in the comics.

    Playing Activities

    1. Choose an activity: Scroll to the "Activities" section on the home screen.
    2. Start Playing: Follow the on-screen instructions to start the activity. Each activity is designed to be intuitive and educational.

    FAQs

    How do I reset the application?

    You can reset the application settings from the Help section by clicking on "Reset App."

    How do I update the app?

    Visit our official website and download the latest version. Follow the installation instructions to update your app.
